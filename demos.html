Demos

In addition to the tutorials, we provide a few more demos to illustrate the capabilities of the pygwb package. These differ from the tutorials in the sense that they are more specific and show how to customize some of the pygwb functionalities to the user’s needs.


Tip

If you are feeling overwhelmed with these demos, make sure to have a look to the tutorials again.
